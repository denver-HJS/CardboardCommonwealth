Cardboard Commonwealth is a record label for the truly creatives. If you've ever needed bust out that tune that's been stuck on your head for days but don't have the equipment to spawn that earworm, then we're the place for you. We offer a cheap (as in well, free) service for laying these PH-phat tracks down.

Here at Cardboard Commonwealth we strive to foster a community of artists. It's always been about the music and the power it has to speak to us on an individual en masse. It's with that in mind we spend the time, energy, and effort to compile professional quality recordings for deserving artists who could otherwise be spending that money on delicious pizza. (Yes, we're talking about YOU!)
